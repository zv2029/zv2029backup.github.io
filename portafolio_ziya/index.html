About Me

Hi, I'm Ziya. I am an NYU student, double majoring in Literature and in Interactive Media. I have numerous interests and passions, one of them being the constant pursuit of interlinking my love for the words on the pages and coding creatively. I have a creative vision of exploring new ways to tell stories through technology. My current focus is to experiment with new forms of narrative expression through code, whether they be web development, or game development, or something different.
